Intro

Data analyst with years of robust experience as a small business retailer and e-commerce director. I have spent years constructing actionable and strategic methods to push for sales efficiency and intelligence in building a retail and e-commerce business. Utilizing data to pull the right metrics for making decisions at various levels of the company, it was also essential to provide data-driven support for stakeholders and leveraging trust in investors. Deploying data models and reports, producing impactful visualizations, and creating actionable strategies for tackling business challenges were essential. While I had been utilizing a tremendous amount of Microsoft Excel (and minimal SQL), Thinkful allowed for me to further develop analytic skills with SQL, Tableau, and Python. Excited to share my work. Please feel free to check out my projects. ENJOY!!.

About

A small business retailer and e-commerce specialist turned data analyst. I'm a strategic thinker and passionate learner certified from Thinkful for Data Analytics. With constructive and concise communication skills, I seek powerful stories and visualizations with data for performance improvement and trend forecasting. I am passionate for research, conflict management, discipline, leadership, and the tenacity to stay on top with profitable results. Having been a small business retailer for many years, it was imperative that I run test methods and utilize data as leverage to gain capital to further grow more business locations and construct actionable improvements within stores to boost revenues. However, my focus and application of data into the development of venturing into online sales transformed me into an e-commerce strategist. The data skillsets and business acumen acquired over the years with my retail background, and immersion in product management, have all drawn me to seek bigger challenges with my data capabilities. I made the decision to join Thinkful bootcamp to ensure I had the right skillsets to take my data journey to the next level. My focus is targeted at developing actionable recommendations for business challenges with data utilizing Excel, Tableau, SQL, and Python. Current hobbies include weight training, cycling (I'm a big fan of Zwift app), reading, and bulletproof coffee. Happy to be your analytical decision support!
